Formal Bio

Supasinee Ardjai, RN, MBA (Graduate)
Risk Management Nurse, Bangkok Hospital Phuket

Supasinee Ardjai is an experienced Registered Nurse and Risk Management Specialist with a strong background in hospital quality assurance and patient safety. She has held progressive leadership roles in nursing and healthcare quality at Bangkok Hospital Phuket and Dibuk Hospital, serving as Head of Nursing and Head of Quality Center before transitioning into her current role as Risk Management Nurse.

Her expertise includes hospital risk governance, clinical safety systems, and the integration of quality management frameworks in healthcare organizations. She has extensive experience leading quality improvement projects, risk prioritization, and implementing patient safety strategies across multiple hospital units.

Supasinee earned her Bachelor of Nursing Science from Thammasat University and later completed a Nursing Management Program at Chulalongkorn University. She is currently pursuing a Master of Business Administration (MBA) at Ramkhamhaeng University, focusing on healthcare leadership and strategic management.

With a career spanning over two decades, Supasinee brings a unique combination of clinical expertise, risk management leadership, and business acumen, making her a valuable contributor to advancing hospital quality and patient safety in Thailand.
